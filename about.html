Who We Are

PRO CAD Steel Engineering is a structural steel detailing and design firm supporting fabricators, contractors, and developers on projects of every scale. We combine disciplined engineering practice with modern detailing software to turn architectural intent into accurate, buildable steel.

Our team works across the full structural steel lifecycle — from early structural design and analysis through detailed shop drawings and connection design — so our clients get one consistent, accountable partner from concept to erection.
